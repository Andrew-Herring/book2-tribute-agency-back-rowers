Career

Banksy has quite the reputation for being unknown, but how does soemone whos so unknown, have such a great career history? Banksy uses his art to portray his political and personal beliefs and thats all he wants them to do. He stricly wants nothing to do with auctioning any of his work, as hes gone as far as shredding his own art work during a liuve auction. Talk about sticking up for your beliefs.

Banksy started doing freehand work for Bristols DBZ (DryBeardZ Crew) with two other artist known as Kato and Tes in the early 1990's. As banksy got more and more into street art, he switched over to Stenciling. Many think it's becasue its easier and less time consuming. It is also said that Banksy toured around Mexico with the Easton Cowboys and Cowgirls in 2001... Whether this is true or not i dont know, but it was on the internet so it must be true!
